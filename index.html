East West University (EWU) is growing steadily upholding the spirits of its oath “Excellence in Education” in order to contribute to the development of the business and technological sectors of Bangladesh. Our programs are updated continuously by an independent academic council consisting of scholars from renowned academic institutions of the country and are based on curricula North American Universities. Our graduates are much sought after and employed by the corporate sectors of the country. EWU has already been recognized as one of the top five Universities of Bangladesh in terms of providing quality education and preparation of graduates equipped to meet the challenges of globalization of business in the 21st Century. Our aim is not only to be the best university in the country but also to be the best in the South Asian region by providing quality education. Post Graduation in Diploma

The Post Graduate Diploma in Entrepreneurship Development (PGDED) aims to practically train individuals to build their skills across several areas which are essential ingredients for creating and running a successful business enterprise. The goal is to create socially responsible entrepreneurs who will not only establish business ventures and be self-employed, but also create jobs for the youths of Bangladesh and make positive contributions to the economy. The PGDED program includes hands-on workshops, training, and seminars conducted by renowned academicians, seasoned entrepreneurs, and industry practitioners. Besides, field visits and case studies from real-life businesses will enhance the students’ problem-solving skills which will better prepare them to manage and run their business enterprises. In the second semester, the students will work on their business idea and develop a business plan. Then they will have the opportunity to pitch their ideas to investors in the investment fair organized by the program. East West University will also provide seed money for the outstanding project(s) as part of its social responsibility to promote new entrepreneurs.

Program Objectives

EWU is committed to providing quality education and to nurturing creativity for producing successful graduates who will serve our society and the world.The primary mission of EWU is to become a center of excellence for higher education, making quality postsecondary education accessible to a larger section of the community at an affordable cost, leading to wide dissemination of educational opportunities, advancement of knowledge creation and promotion of economic, social and technological progress. EWU will excel in critical disciplines that address socio-economic challenges nationally and globally, and advance creative pursuits necessary for innovative solutions to societal issues. EWU will provide a stimulating learning environment in which the faculty and students can discover, critically examine, preserve and transmit knowledge, values and wisdom to benefit society and improve the quality of life for all. Through its academic and administrative policies and programs, EWU will ensure that its students receive the highest quality of education, preparing them for a purposeful life, personally, professionally and socially.
